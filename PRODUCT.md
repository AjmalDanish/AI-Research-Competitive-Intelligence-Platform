# AI Website Intelligence Platform

## Vision

Build a production-grade AI platform capable of understanding any website and transforming it into structured business intelligence.

The long-term vision is to create an AI research and competitive intelligence platform that can automatically analyze companies, products, markets, and technologies from publicly available websites.

This project is designed as a flagship open-source portfolio project demonstrating advanced AI Engineering, Software Engineering, Data Engineering, and Machine Learning skills.

---

# Problem Statement

Business analysts spend significant time manually reviewing company websites to understand:

- What the company does
- Products and services
- Technology stack
- Contact information
- Industry
- Competitors
- Market positioning

This process is repetitive, slow, and difficult to scale.

Modern Large Language Models can automate much of this work when combined with reliable web crawling and structured extraction.

---

# Solution

The AI Website Intelligence Platform automates website understanding by combining:

- Web Crawling
- Content Extraction
- Natural Language Processing
- Technology Detection
- Large Language Models
- Structured Data Extraction

The result is clean business intelligence in a machine-readable format.

---

# Non-Goals

The MVP will NOT include:

- Authentication
- User Management
- Dashboards
- Reporting
- Billing
- Notifications
- Mobile Apps
- Frontend UI

These are future enhancements.

---

# Success Criteria

The MVP is considered successful when it can:

- Crawl websites reliably
- Extract clean content
- Detect company information
- Identify technologies
- Generate useful AI summaries
- Return structured JSON through a REST API
- Store results in PostgreSQL
